Visa and Citizenship requests:

Do you want to access documents or details you provided to the Department?

  1. ImmiAccount gives clients immediate access to e-lodged application forms, supporting documentation and departmental correspondence, such as grant letters.
    • If you'd like a copy of your visa application for your own records or your original application was made through ImmiAccount, please login to your ImmiAccount
    • If you’d like to access your Document for Travel to Australia (DFTTA), apply for an ImmiCard.
    • If you'd like a copy of your permanent residency visa or current visa status for your own records and are a long term resident who arrived in Australia before 1990 and have not left the country since your arrival, apply for an ImmiCard.
  2. If you'd like to request to cancel a Temporary Resident visa for you or your partner, please complete form 1194 Certification of Immigration Status

Do you want to access documents or details owned by the Department?

  1. If you’d like to request evidence of your current visa status as proof of permanent residency, permission or visa status, or to apply for work or Medicare or to update details for a new request, visit Visa Entitlement Verification Online (VEVO).
  2. If you'd like to request a copy of your Australian Citizen Certificate for your own records, please complete form 119 Application for evidence of Australian citizenship.
  3. If you'd like to request a visa grant number for your own records, you will need call the DATACOM staff and provide proof of your identity .
  4. If you'd like to request evidence of a deceased person’s citizenship status for your own records, please complete form 1391 Request for confirmation of Australian citizenship status of a deceased person.
  5. If you'd like to request a copy of your International Movement information for your own records, please complete form 1359 Request for international movement records.
  6. If you'd like to request a copy of your Certificate of Immigration Status as proof of Superannuation Status, please complete form 1194 Certification of Immigration Status.

Do you want to update your documents or details owned by the Department?

If you’d like to request an update be made to your contact details (address, passport name, contact details or gender or you wish to provide updated identity (marriage, birth certificate) or business documents to the Department, please refer to the Changes in Situation page of the website.

If you think the information the Department owns about you is:

  • is incomplete, incorrect, out of date or misleading
  • has been, is being, or is available to be used for an administrative purpose
