  1. If you'd like to request evidence of current visa status to provide proof for another government agency or employer, visit Visa Entitlement Verification Online (VEVO).
  2. If you'd like to request a copy of your visa application for your own records and are a long term resident who arrived in Australia before 1990 and have not left the country since your arrival, visit IMMI.
  3. If you'd like to request a visa grant number for your own records, visit ???
  4. If you'd like to apply for ImmiCard for ???
  5. If you'd like to request evidence of a deceased person’s citizenship status for your own records GO TO …
  6. If you'd like to request a copy of your Australian Citizen Certificate for your own records • GO TO … Evidence of your Australian Citizenship, please complete form 119 Application for evidence of Australian citizenship.
  7. If you'd like to request a copy of your International Movement information for your own records • GO TO … Your International Movement records, please complete form 1359 Request for international movement records.
  8. If you'd like to request a copy of your Immigration Status Certificate for your own records • GO TO … Your superannuation status, please complete form 1194 Certification of Immigration Status and/or request to cancel a Temporary Resident visa.
  9. If you'd like to request a copy of your Immigration Status Certificate as proof of Superannuation Status • GO TO … Your superannuation status, please complete form 1194 Certification of Immigration Status and/or request to cancel a Temporary Resident visa.
  10. If you'd like to request to cancel a Temporary Resident visa for your own records • GO TO … Your superannuation status, please complete form 1194 Certification of Immigration Status and/or request to cancel a Temporary Resident visa.
