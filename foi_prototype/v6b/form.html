Be as specific as possible when describing the documents or information to help us quickly locate what you want to access:

Sending a vague request with missing ‘scope’ may add a substantial delay to the finalisation of your FOI request. This is because the information may include paper files which may be stored across the country, and given the COVID-19 pandemic, currently accessing hard copies of files can be difficult.

Avoid using non-specific language or broad terminology such as:

  • “all visa files”
  • “all documents relating to the applicant”
  • “including but not limited to..”
  • “other such documents”
  • “and other relevant files”
  • “Anything that appears on the system under my name and DOB”

Under section 15(2) of the Freedom of Information Act 1982, I am seeking access to the following document(s).
