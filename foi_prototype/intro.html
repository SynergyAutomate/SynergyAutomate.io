Is there a quicker way to access information?

You do not need to make an FOI request if you only want copies of your own evidence or certificates as there are alternative (and faster) ways to access this information.

We now have some questions to help you step through to options that may be what you are looking for.

Visa and immigration - copies of your documents

Do you want to access your personal visa and citizenship information or application?

This could be for to access your own records, immediate access to e-lodged application forms, supporting documentation and departmental correspondence, such as grant letters or your original application you made through ImmiAccount.
Log in to your ImmiAccount

Do you want to access your Document for Travel to Australia (DFTTA)?

Apply for an ImmiCard

Do you want a copy of your permanent residency visa or current visa status?

This applies to long term residents who arrived in Australia before 1990 and have not left the country since arriving.
Apply for an ImmiCard

Do you want to cancel a Temporary Resident visa  for you or your partner?

Please complete form 1194 Certification of Immigration Status.

Do you want to access your personal records in order to prepare your application for a subsequent Temporary Protection visa (TPV) or Safe Haven Enterprise visa (SHEV)?

Please complete the Admin Release form.

Do you want a copy of your visa documentation to share with a new migration agent?

Migration Agents must retain documents/records for 7 years and provide a copy of the documentation to you within 7 days (refer to the Migration Agents Requirements Act).
Please contact your previous migration agent

Do you want a copy of your Australian Citizen Certificate for your own records?

Please complete form 119 Application for evidence of Australian citizenship

Do you want a copy of a  visa grant number for your own records?

Please call the DATACOM staff and provide proof of your identity

Do you want to access evidence of a deceased person’s citizenship status for your own records?

Please complete form 1391 Request for confirmation of Australian citizenship status of a deceased person

Do you want a copy of your International Movement information for your own records?

Please complete form 1359 Request for international movement records

Do you want a copy of your Certificate of Immigration Status as proof of  Superannuation Status?

Please complete form 1194 Certification of Immigration Status

Visa and immigration – status of applications in progress

Do you want to check evidence of your current visa status?

This could be to prove permanent residency, permission or visa status, or to apply for work or Medicare or to update details for a new request.
Visa Entitlement Verification Online system (VEVO) allows visa holders, employers, education providers and other organisations to check visa conditions.

Updating documents or details held by the Department

Do you want to update details or provide updated identity documents to the Department?

This could be updating address, passport name, contact details or gender or a new identity document (marriage, birth certificate)
Please refer to the Changes in Situation page of the website.
