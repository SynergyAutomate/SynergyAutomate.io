Use this list to find out if your access request is quicker by a different channel.

Is there a quicker way to access information?

You do not need to make an FOI request if you only want copies of your own evidence or certificates as there are alternative (and quicker) ways to access this information.

We now have some questions to help you step through to options that may be what you are looking for.

Access to documents or details you provided to the Department

Are you wanting to access your personal visa and citizenship information or application?

This could be for to access your own records, immediate access to e-lodged application forms, supporting documentation and departmental correspondence, such as grant letters or your original application you made through ImmiAccount.
Log in to your ImmiAccount

Are you wanting to access your Document for Travel to Australia (DFTTA)?

Apply for an ImmiCard.

Are you wanting to access a copy of your permanent residency visa or current visa status for your own records and are a long term resident who arrived in Australia before 1990 and have not left the country since your arrival?

Apply for an ImmiCard.

If you'd like to request to cancel a Temporary Resident visa  for you or your partner?

Please complete form 1194 Certification of Immigration Status.

Are you wanting to access your personal records in order to prepare your application for a subsequent Temporary Protection visa (TPV) or Safe Haven Enterprise visa (SHEV)?

Please complete the Admin Release form.

Are you wanting to access a copy of your visa documentation to share with a new migration agent?

Please contact your previous migration agent as Migration Agents must retain documents/records for 7 years and provide a copy of the documentation to you within 7 days (refer to the Migration Agents Requirements Act).

Access to documents or details held by the Department ?

Are you wanting to check evidence of your current visa status ?

This could be to prove permanent residency, permission or visa status, or to apply for work or Medicare or to update details for a new request.
Visa Entitlement Verification Online system (VEVO) allows visa holders, employers, education providers and other organisations to check visa conditions.

Are you wanting to access a copy of your Australian Citizen Certificate for your own records?

Please complete form 119 Application for evidence of Australian citizenship.

Are you wanting to request a  visa grant number for your own records?

Please call the DATACOM staff and provide proof of your identity (what are the details?).

Are you wanting to access evidence of a deceased person’s citizenship status for your own records?

Please complete form 1391 Request for confirmation of Australian citizenship status of a deceased person.

Are you wanting to access a copy of your International Movement information for your own records?

Please complete form 1359 Request for international movement records.

Are you wanting to access a copy of your Certificate of Immigration Status as proof of  Superannuation Status ?

Please complete form 1194 Certification of Immigration Status

Updating documents or details held by the Department

Are you wanting an update be made to contact details (address, passport name, contact details or gender or you wish to provide updated identity (marriage, birth certificate) or business documents to the Department?

Please refer to the Changes in Situation page of the website.

Do you think the information the Department owns about you is incomplete, incorrect, out of date or misleading and has been, is being, or is available to be used for an administrative purpose?

Please complete form 424c Request for amendment or annotation to personal records.
